ZeroSpam protects your business against ransomware, phishing, phishing, and denial of service. ZeroSpam also protects it against spam as well as messages containing attachments and dangerous links.

Legitimate messages

ZeroSpam ensures that you receive all the legitimate messages that are intended for your business. In this way you will receive your orders, contracts and all e-mails from your customers, business partners and employees securely.

A security arsenal

ZeroSpam also includes a complete arsenal of security measures that prevent redundancy, protect confidentiality, create an automatic queue (queuing) in case of customer failure and protect the users of your systems.
