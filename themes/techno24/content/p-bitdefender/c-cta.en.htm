Bitdefender also protects your company's systems against viruses, rootkit, malware and phishing. It also protects against intrusions and loss of sensitive data.

Update and administration

All Bitdefender updates are in the background, without ever slowing the productivity of your business. The administration of your Bitedefender is centralized on an online management console. You create your own access profiles and assign them the rights according to the policies and procedures in effect in your company.
