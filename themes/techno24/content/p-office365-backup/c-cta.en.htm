With Office 365 Backup, data is backed up and recoverable in just a few clicks

With 4 automatic backups per day, unlimited storage and retention, and simple, granular recovery, you can be assured that you are proactively protecting your customers’ data

What’s backed up?

  • Exchange Online: mailboxes, Archives, folders, calendars, contacts, permissions and more.
  • SharePoint Online: site collections, lists, libraries, folders, metadata, security settings and version history.
  • Groupes Microsoft 365: group files, conversations, notes, sites and more.
  • Microsoft Teams: channels, conversations, work files, meeting items, and more.
  • OneDrive for Business: lists, libraries, folders, items, metadata, security settings and version history.
  • Public Folders: mailboxes, messages, files, contacts, forms and validations.
  • Project Online: project plans, tasks and files.
  • Planner: Tasks, buckets, progression, labels and assignments.
  • Dynamics 365 CRM: entities, archive documents, forms and field attributes without losing the corresponding metadata.
  • Salesforce: Backup at the organization, object, record and field levels.
